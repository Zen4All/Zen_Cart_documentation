Before you make a decision about which upgrade you wish to do (Standard or Database-only), take a look at the Reference Guide. It contains a lot of useful details about things that are important to know about upgrading.

Upgrading - Options

Detailed upgrade instructions that help you retain your customizations while upgrading to new features, see Standard Upgrade - Recommended Procedure.
